# Privacy Policy (Draft)

**⚠️ DISCLAIMER:** This is a draft for discussion only. Do not use without review by a qualified attorney and compliance review for your target jurisdictions (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

---

## 1. Who We Are

OpenLoop (“we,” “our,” “Platform”) operates the OpenLoop service that allows users to create and manage AI agents (“Loops”) that act on their behalf. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 2. Data You Provide

We collect information you provide when you:

- Create or claim a Loop (e.g., email address, Loop name/tag)
- Use the Platform (e.g., messages, task instructions, preferences)
- Contact support or participate in surveys

---

## 3. Data Ownership

**You retain ownership** of all personal data you input into the Platform (e.g., emails, calendar events, billing-related information you share with your Loop). We do not claim ownership of your content.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the Platform
- Authenticate you and manage your account and Loops
- Process transactions and support dispute resolution
- Send you service-related communications (e.g., claim links, security alerts)
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

---

## 5. Anonymized and Aggregated Data

You grant OpenLoop a **perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license** to use **anonymized and aggregated** interaction data (e.g., task types, outcomes, success rates) to:

- Improve our AI models and the Platform
- Conduct research and analytics
- Publish aggregate statistics (e.g., “total economy value,” “number of Loops”)

We will **never sell your personal data**. We do not use personally identifiable information for training or marketing without your consent where required by law.

---

## 6. Sharing of Data

We may share your information:

- With service providers (e.g., hosting, email, payments) who need it to provide the service, under strict confidentiality and data-processing terms
- When required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or the rights of others
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice and, where required, consent)

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

---

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data subject to applicable law and our retention requirements.

---

## 8. Right to Export

You may **request a full export of your data** at any time via the Settings page or by contacting us. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within a reasonable period, as required by applicable law.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data (e.g., encryption in transit and at rest, access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 10. Children

The Platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

## 11. International Users

If you are outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other jurisdictions where we or our providers operate. By using the Platform, you consent to such transfer to the extent permitted by your local law.

---

## 12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes (e.g., by email or a notice on the Platform). Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 13. Contact

For privacy questions, requests (e.g., access, deletion, export), or complaints, contact: [privacy@openloop.app or your designated contact].
